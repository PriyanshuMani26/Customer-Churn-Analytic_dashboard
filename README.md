 Telecom Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard
 ---------------------------------------------
 Project Objective
 ===================
 To analyze customer churn behavior in a telecom environment by identifying key drivers across Internet and Phone services and developing an executive Power BI dashboard to support data-driven retention strategies.

#Project Summary
====================
This project investigates customer churn patterns using service-level, behavioral, and revenue-based metrics. The analysis focuses on factors such as tenure, contract type, payment method (electronic check, card payments), monthly charges, and ARPU.

Key findings include:
	•	High churn concentration among short-tenure customers
	•	Elevated churn in electronic payment segments
	•	Lower churn among bundled and long-tenure customers
	•	Revenue risk driven by specific customer segments
An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to visualize churn trends, segment high-risk customers, and quantify revenue impact, enabling strategic retention and pricing decisions.

Dataset Information
========================
Include:
	•	Source :Kaggle (Original source IBM)
	•	Number of records :147924
	•	Key features:
  The data set includes information about:
   1) Customers who left within the last month – the column is called Churn
   2)Services that each customer has signed up for – phone, multiple lines, internet, online security, online backup, device protection, tech support, and streaming TV and movies
   3)Customer account information – how long they’ve been a customer, contract, payment method, paperless billing, monthly charges, and total charges
   4)Demographic info about customers – gender, age range, and if they have partners and dependents

#Key Metrics Used
===================
	•	Churn Rate
	•	ARPU
	•	Revenue at Risk
	•	Subscriber Churn Index
  •	Customers & Contract Drivers
	•	Service Impact On Churn

#Key Insights
====================
  •	Short-tenure customers exhibit the highest churn rate
	•	Electronic check users show elevated churn compared to automated payments
	•	Month-to-month contracts drive higher churn than long-term contracts
	•	Bundled service customers demonstrate higher retention and ARPU
	•	Streaming services shows lowest performance hence needs special attention.
